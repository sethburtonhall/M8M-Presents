In it's simplest form M8M Presents is a film club covering a wide spectrum of genres & budgets. But, at it's core, in the depths of its soul, M8M Presents is much, much more. Our mission is to strengthen the vibrant creative community in the Triad, through breaking of bread and creative discussion. M8M Presents also exists to inform others about the many projects being produced by Middle8Media & Highway 29 Motion Pictures and to invite others to join our film collective.
