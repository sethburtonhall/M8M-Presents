Welcome

In it's simplest form Middle8Media Presents is a film club, a venue for film that covers a wide spectrum of genres & budgets. But, at it's core, in the depths of its soul, it is much, much more. M8M Presents exists to strengthen the vibrant community that surrounds us every day, through gathering, breaking of bread and creative discussion. M8M Presents also exists to inform others about the many projects being produced by Middle8Media & Highway 29 Motion Pictures, all right here in the Triad and invite anyone interested to join our film collective.
